# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Robin is operated by a trusted owner or demo operator from a private control surface while meeting participants interact with Robin through ordinary Zoom audio. Operators need immediate situational awareness, unmistakable approval boundaries, and a reliable path to stop automation or take over the Mac.

## Product Purpose

Robin turns a dedicated Mac and signed-in Zoom Workplace account into an agentic coworker. Success means it can join, converse, carry out reversible desktop work, share only the intended workspace, recover safely, and produce a useful redacted trace without exposing secrets or unrelated content.

## Brand Personality

Calm, vigilant, and plainspoken. Robin should feel like a capable operations partner: warm enough to be human, restrained enough to inspire trust, and emphatic only when safety or intervention requires attention.

## Anti-references

Avoid consumer-chat whimsy, sci-fi command-center theatrics, generic SaaS metric-card dashboards, decorative AI gradients, and security controls hidden behind menus. The interface must never make model activity look more certain or more authorized than it is.

## Design Principles

- Make the next safe action obvious.
- Show evidence and state, not theatrical intelligence.
- Separate observation, action, approval, and external commitment.
- Keep emergency stop and takeover continuously reachable.
- Prefer predictable controls over novel interaction patterns.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA, full keyboard operation, visible focus, non-color state cues, readable live regions, reduced-motion support, and touch targets suitable for a remote operator under time pressure.
